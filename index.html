The Story

Welcome to the FOLD

In a quiet town near Boston, one family's perfect life is shattered when a night at the movies ends in tragedy. Eight-year-old Jamie Thornton mysteriously vanishes, leaving her father, Jim, desperate for answers and the world he knew in ruins.

When the justice system fails, Jim is approached by a secretive group called the FOLD: a network of grieving fathers promising justice outside the law. Drawn into their seductive pledge, Jim spirals into a dark underworld where nothing is as it seems. The truth always out of view, hidden below the surface.

As the FOLD's true motives are revealed, Jim uncovers a chilling conspiracy that connects shadowy government forces, staged lies, and a sinister elite determined to exploit him. Fueled by desperation, Jim teams up with an unlikely ally, only to uncover an even more shocking truth:

Jamie may still be alive.

Racing against time, Jim must navigate betrayal, deadly secrets, and his own morality to bring his daughter home.

Welcome to the FOLD. Where justice has its own rules.

The Author

Greg Loeffelholz

Author · Thriller Writer

Greg Loeffelholz is a debut thriller writer with a gift for weaving pulse-pounding suspense with deeply human stories. Rooted in the atmosphere of the Boston area, with its rain-slicked streets, old neighborhoods, and raw grit. The FOLD is his first novel in what promises to be a gripping series. It's the kind of story driven by the questions that keep us up at night: about justice, family, and how far we'd go when the system abandons us.

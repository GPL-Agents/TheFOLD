Official Synopsis

In a quiet town near Boston, one family's perfect life is shattered when a night at the movies ends in tragedy — eight-year-old Jamie Thornton mysteriously vanishes, leaving her father, Jim, desperate for answers and the world he knew in ruins.

When the justice system fails, Jim is approached by a secretive group called the FOLD — a network of grieving fathers promising justice outside the law. Drawn into their seductive pledge, Jim spirals into a dark underworld where nothing is as it seems. As the FOLD's true motives are revealed, Jim uncovers a chilling conspiracy that connects shadowy government forces, staged lies, and a sinister elite determined to exploit him.

Fueled by desperation, Jim teams up with an unlikely ally, only to uncover an even more shocking truth: Jamie may still be alive. Racing against time, Jim must navigate betrayal, deadly secrets, and his own morality to bring his daughter home.

Welcome to the FOLD — where justice has its own rules.
